All children will be treated as part of our family while in our home. Each day will be filled with structured activities as well as adequate time for fun play with other children. All children are fed 2 home made nutritious meals and 2 snacks per day. Infants thru 12 years old are welcome.

Indoor Play Area

At Lia Maria Daycare children take extreme pride in the various activities and materials used during indoor play. Their interests and imaginations are enhanced as each child engages in the many learning play stations throughout the play areas. Infants and toddlers improve motor skills as they roll the big ball back and forth on a play mat. All play materials and activities are age appropriate and cleaned regularly. The play area is well organized with many bins of toys, books, puzzles and activities that the children are quite familiar with. They are encouraged to play with any appropriate items, but also taught to share toys with other children and return toys to their proper places when they are done with them.

Your child's safety and health is our main concern. We do not use physical or mental discipline of any kind with the children. We focus on use of positive praise, re-directing, time-outs, separation (based on child's age) and discussion as tools for guidance and discipline.

Outdoor Play Area

We like to spend time outdoors as often as weather permits each day that the children are here. We are happy to offer a newly constructed fenced in play area for children of all ages. The use of safe and age appropriate play equipments allows the children to get the necessary physical exercise needed for growth and youth development. The entire play area is on top of on four inches of rubber Kinder Mulch that provided a soft and safe surface for the occasional misstep on the climbing toys.

Dining Area

Family style dining is what we prefer and is an important part of our day. Not only do we learn many life skills, but family style dining also embraces the cozy home like atmosphere that we strive to offer here everyday. All food is placed in child size serving bowls or plates on the table and the children are encouraged to serve themselves or serve themselves with help from an adult. We sit at the table with the children, and we enjoy meaningful conversations together in a pleasant mealtime atmosphere, much like the children do at home with their families.

We provide meals and snacks that include a variety of food groups including meat and meat alternates, bread and grains, fruits, vegetables and milk. Our menus rotate on a four-week schedule to provide variety for your child. Your children are given homemade meals made with fresh, wholesome ingredients.

Quiet Area

We have designated areas used for naptime. Based on the schedule children will be going to nap at or around 1:00 pm. Each child will be put in pack-n-play cribs, cots or full bed based on their age. The sheets will be washed once a week or as needed. If your child has a toy or a blanket they like to sleep with you are welcome to bring it. We will be playing relaxing or classical music for the children during the nap time.

Quality

Forming positive relationships between the caregiver, parent, and child is essential to providing quality care. The parent is free to visit the child care program at all times and will be notified and made aware of any problems that may arise. Feel free to discuss any concerns with us so that we can provide your child exactly what he or she needs. Open lines of communication and knowing what occurs in your child’s day to day activities will give you a sense comfort. Our daycare is a mixed age licensed daycare home. We care for newborns through school age children and offer full and part time services. The daycare is open from 6:00 am to 6:00 pm Monday through Friday, and we are closed on holidays. At our daycare there are always two adults who will supervise the children.
